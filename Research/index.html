Research projects

An integrative framework for explaining behavioral and fMRI data together.

How do you deal with your behavioral data when you conduct neuroimaging studies? How can you identify any neural correlates of your computational mechanisms? Such questions motivated the field of model-based computational neuroscience, and my major work in my graduate school was to develop an integrative approach to explain behavioral and fMRI data together.

A generative framework for modeling behavioral and eye fixation data together

Eye-tracking provides a proxy of attention mechanism during human decision making. Can we use current computational models to explain both attention and behavioral data?
