About

I'm a Ph.D. student in the Ohio State University, working with Dr. Brandon M. Turner. I'm interested in exploring different cognitive models about decision-making and memory. I've been devoted to constructing mechanistic models on a particular value-based decision making task - intertemporal choice. I'm also engaged in modeling brain connectivities and combining the neural results with psychological behavioral data. My second degree in process is the applied statistic master and I'm most interested in the stochastic statistics.
